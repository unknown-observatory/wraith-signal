# Product Brief

Wraith Signal is a browser-based exploration game about discovering a mysterious world.

The product should feel simple to enter, beautiful to explore, and memorable because of what the player finds.

---

# Product Definition

Wraith Signal is not primarily a farming game, economy simulator, lore wiki, or generic multiplayer sandbox.

It is an exploration-first experience where:

- the world is the main character;
- players search for hidden places, caches, and rare visual moments;
- discovery creates status and memory;
- social sharing supports the experience instead of replacing it.

---

# Target Experience

The first player reaction should be curiosity.

The first successful session should deliver:

- movement through a small atmospheric world;
- at least one meaningful discovery;
- at least one moment of wonder;
- a reason to remember or share the moment.

---

# Product Pillars

## Wonder

The game must create moments players remember.

## Exploration

The core activity is moving through the world and noticing what others miss.

## Discovery Status

Recognition should come from discovery, not grind.

## Simplicity

The MVP should be small enough to finish and polished enough to feel intentional.

## Repository Discipline

Every accepted decision must live in the repository.

---

# Non-Goals

The MVP is not:

- a full MMO;
- a large open world;
- a token farming product;
- a complex economy;
- a platform for many games;
- a complete lore delivery system;
- a generic engine.
