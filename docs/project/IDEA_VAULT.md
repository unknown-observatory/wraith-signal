# Idea Vault

This document stores ideas that should not be lost but are not approved MVP work.

Ideas in this file are not executable scope. To become work, an idea must be refined, approved, and represented by a GitHub Issue.

---

# Product And Economy Ideas

- Token rewards such as `$WRAITH`.
- Vaults containing tokens or other rewards.
- Pump.fun launch strategy.
- Monetization through short-lived, high-attention experiences.
- Wallet claim mechanics.
- Progression tied to discovery status rather than grind.

Notes:

- NFTs are explicitly not part of the reward direction discussed historically.
- Economy must not overpower exploration.

---

# Discovery And Social Ideas

- Proof of Discovery screenshots.
- Social share messages such as "I was first here" or "I was here".
- Explorer records.
- Hall of Explorers or Hall of Memories.
- Historical records from the vanished civilization.
- Community-driven clue interpretation.
- Shared discovery status.

---

# World And Lore Ideas

- The Signal as the underlying mystery.
- Ancient civilization traces.
- Cave images or prehistoric visual storytelling.
- Observatory as a meaningful recurring place.
- Forest secrets layer.
- City or district as a compact future area.
- Wonders with different identities per biome.
- World evolution over time.

---

# Wonder Ideas

- White Stag.
- Celestial or impossible creatures.
- Floating or impossible architecture.
- Biome-specific wonders.
- Rare visual events that are seen briefly and remembered.

Constraint:

- Wonders should not be reused lazily across biomes. A forest Wonder should feel different from a sky, ocean, desert, or city Wonder.

---

# Systems To Reconsider Later

- Party-gated discoveries.
- Obstacles that teach players they may need help.
- Reputation systems.
- Achievement systems.
- Discovery records connected to lore.
- Larger world streaming.
- Multiplayer rituals.
- Ongoing seasons or content drops.

---

# Rejected Or High-Risk Directions

These are not permanently banned, but they are high-risk for the current product:

- Farm-first gameplay.
- Economy-first retention.
- Large MMO scope.
- Complex secret alphabets or languages.
- Building an in-house AI studio platform.
- Building a generic engine before the game needs it.
